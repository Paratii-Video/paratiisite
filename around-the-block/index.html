About

Around the Block is a 6-episode documentary series about blockchain technologists who are decentralising the engines of modern society.

It's production is spearheaded by one of Brazil's biggest film companies, and launch is scheduled for Q4 2017. Content will be released on Paratii, a decentralised peer-to-peer video player under development.

Sergio
Lerner

Security researcher, co-founder of RSK Labs, author of the Lumino-Compression Transaction Protocol

Sergio has found multiple vulnerabilities in Bitcoin Core and extensively contributed to its codebase. A proponent of off-chain scaling, he's the Chief Scientist @ RSK Labs, which intends to bring smart contracts to Bitcoin.

Alex Van
de Sande

Designer and frontend developer, team lead for Mist (the Ethereum browser)

Alex proposed Mist still as an Ethereum fan, and has since become the project's lead. He's also part of the team who brought us ENS, the Ethereum Name System, and is widely recognised as one of the most respectable voices in the community.

Aron
Fischer

Maths PhD, engineer @ Colony and Swarm

Specialized in Algebraic Topology and Homotopy theory, Aron is developing the governance protocols @ Colony, and working on state and payment channels for the Ethereum Foundation’s Swarm incentive structure.

Viktor
Trón

Ethereum core developer, team lead for Swarm

A linguist with long academic records, Viktor has shifted focus towards building the data layer for web 3.0 a few years ago, as one of Ethereum's first employees.

Simon de
la Rouviere

Blockchain developer @ Consensys

A South African self proclaimed "engineer of societies", Simon's time is shared among coding for UJO, a decentralised music distribution platform; conceiving novel models for curation and content monetisation; and working for and Consensys.

Anthony di Iorio & Charlie Shrem

Co-founder of Ethereum, Co-founder of BitInstant, CEO and COO @ Jaxx

Charlie's story is mixed with that of Bitcoin itself. Currently leading operations @ Jaxx, he's been through a conviction, mainstream fame and a series of crypto businesses. Former Chief Digital Officer of the Toronto Stock Exchange, di Iorio was largely responsible for funding early development of Ethereum before leading Jaxx and Decentral.

Everton
Fraga

Software engineer @ the Ethereum foundation

Everton is the second Brazilian addition to Ethereum's official team, and has been contributing with the development of Mist, the platform's browser.

Gavin
Wood

Former CTO of Ethereum, founder of Parity Technologies, inventor of Polkadot

Gavin coded the first functional implementation of Ethereum, invented the Solidity contract language and wrote the Yellow Paper - first formal specification of any blockchain protocol and one of the key ways Ethereum distinguishes itself.

Don Tapscott

Co-founder @ Blockchain Research Institute, author of 15 books and world-known authority on decentralisation

Don wrote "The Blockchain Revolution" and gave the most watched Ted Talk ever on descentralization. Through a research institute, he's helping governments and corporate entities around the world use blockchains to create civic participation and improve political or business processes.

Alexey
Akhunov

Ethereum developer, former banking software specialist

Russian-born, security-oriented, Alexey is a PhD in Computer Sciences who's been voluntarily contributing to the Ethereum ecosystem.

Ronaldo
Lemos

Father of Brazil's Marco Civil Law of the Internet, former professor @ Columbia University

Director for the Institute of Technology and Society in Rio de Janeiro, Ronaldo has spearheaded the launch of Mudamos, an Ethereum-based application for pushing qualifying law proposals to the chamber and encouraging civic participation in Brazil.

Victor
Maia

Software engineer and programming language design researcher

Part of the Mist (Ethereum browser) team, Victor has been following the vision of bringing it to the masses. His work on Moon, a novel language for writing faster dApps, is a recent effort in this direction.
